About Us

Afterglow is a student-led club dedicated to supporting postpartum mothers through care packages, awareness campaigns, and community outreach. We believe in spreading warmth, comfort, and connection to those who need it most.

Warmth Kits

We assemble care packages with essentials for new mothers.

Awareness Events

Hosting info sessions to educate about postpartum wellness.

Community Outreach

Partnering with local hospitals to offer support programs.
