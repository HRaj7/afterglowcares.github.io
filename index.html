About the Cause

The Afterglow Club is a student-run organization dedicated to supporting postpartum mothers. Our mission is to provide care, compassion, and practical support through care packages, community events, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with local hospitals and volunteers. We aim to spread warmth and meaningful assistance to new mothers.
