Afterglow Cares Club raises awareness about postpartum health and creates care packages to support mothers in our community.

See Our Work

About Us

We are a student-led club dedicated to supporting postpartum women by raising awareness and giving back through hands-on community projects. Each meeting includes a project that helps new mothers or brings kindness to our local community.

“Because every mother deserves care after giving care.”

Our Projects

We create postpartum care packages filled with essentials, self-care items, and handwritten messages. Our members also organize donation drives and volunteer with local women’s centers.
