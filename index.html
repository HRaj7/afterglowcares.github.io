Supporting Postpartum Mothers with Care & Compassion

Afterglow Club provides warmth, encouragement, and practical support to new mothers during the critical postpartum period.

About the Cause

The Afterglow Club is a student-run organization dedicated to supporting postpartum mothers. Our mission is to provide care, compassion, and practical support through care packages, community events, awareness campaigns, and partnerships with local hospitals and volunteers. We strive to spread warmth, encouragement, and meaningful assistance to new mothers when they need it most.
