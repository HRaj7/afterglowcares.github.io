About Us

Afterglow Club is a student-run nonprofit supporting postpartum mothers. We provide care packages, hold community projects, and work to raise awareness about postpartum health.

Our Mission & Approach

We believe every mother deserves support after childbirth. Our programs provide practical resources, emotional encouragement, and a safe community to empower postpartum mothers.
