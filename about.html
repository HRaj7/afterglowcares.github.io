About Us

The Afterglow Club is dedicated to supporting postpartum mothers and raising awareness about postpartum health. We create care packages, host community events, and provide practical support to new mothers.

Our Mission & Approach

We believe every mother deserves support and care during the postpartum period. Our programs provide practical assistance, emotional encouragement, and community engagement. We prioritize inclusivity, empathy, and meaningful outreach in all our activities.
