About Afterglow

Afterglow is a student-run club dedicated to supporting postpartum mothers through care packages, community events, and awareness campaigns. We believe in spreading warmth, love, and practical help to new moms during a crucial time.

Our mission is to provide both emotional and physical support by partnering with local hospitals, volunteers, and community organizations. We aim to create a network where mothers feel seen, supported, and valued.
