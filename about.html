About Us

Afterglow Cares Club is a student-run nonprofit supporting postpartum women. We raise awareness about postpartum health and create care packages to provide real help to mothers in our community.

Our Mission & Approach

Our mission is to support new mothers with care, education, and community. At every meeting, we run projects like assembling care packages that give back and make a meaningful impact on postpartum women and their families.
