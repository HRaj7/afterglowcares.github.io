About Afterglow

We’re a student-led club focused on supporting postpartum mothers through practical help and community. Our volunteers assemble care kits, host educational events, and partner with local health centers to reach families in need.

Our Values

  • Compassion — Every action is focused on care.
  • Community — We partner with local groups and students.
  • Learning — We host events that teach and connect.
