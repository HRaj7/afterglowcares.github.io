About Us

As a student-led non-profit, Afterglow Club is committed to supporting postpartum mothers by spreading warmth, care, and encouragement. Through our projects and partnerships, we aim to bring real impact to our community.

Our Mission & Approach

We believe every mother deserves support during the postpartum period. Our mission is to provide emotional and practical care through packages, events, and awareness programs.
