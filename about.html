About Us

As a non-profit organization, we aim to provide accessible arts and crafts education to underserved children in the Bay Area. Join us in empowering young artists!

Our Mission & Approach

At Afterglow Club, we believe that every child deserves access to support and encouragement. Our programs provide a safe and nurturing environment for new mothers to receive care packages, participate in workshops, and build connections. We prioritize inclusivity, compassion, and practical assistance in all our initiatives.
