About Us

As a non-profit organization, we aim to provide accessible arts and crafts education to underserved children in the Bay Area. Join us in empowering young artists!

Our Mission & Approach

At Afterglow Club, we believe every child deserves access to art education. Our programs provide a safe and nurturing environment for children to channel their creativity and develop new skills. We prioritize diversity and inclusivity in all our programs, ensuring every child feels supported and valued. Let's empower young artists and build a brighter future for our community!
