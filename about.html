About Us

The Afterglow Club is a student-run non-profit dedicated to raising awareness about postpartum health and supporting new mothers through care packages, community events, and educational initiatives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help mothers heal, connect, and thrive after childbirth by providing resources, guidance, and compassion through our community-based programs and student outreach.
